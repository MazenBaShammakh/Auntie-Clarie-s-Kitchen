Welcom to Auntie Clarie's Kitchen

If you're a university student living in-campus, Auntie Clarie's Kitchen is your best choice for homemade, local food.

Auntie Clarie's Kitchen

Welcome to my Kitchen. Auntie Clarie's Kitchen is a home kitchen that prepare delicuous, traditional Malay food for university students who usually suffer from high prices of out-door food. Here, I am really eager to help those students and treat them as my kids!

My Mission

Auntie Clarie's Kitchen mission is to make in-campus students life as if they're in their hometown! Instead of eating same food daily in university's cafetria, students have a chance to be served with a various dishes prepared by Auntie Clarie.
